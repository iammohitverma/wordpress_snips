Health & safety notice

We recommend that the utmost care should always be taken when handling and storage of your swimming pool chemicals. The materials should always be stored separately, sealed in, preferably, their original containers.

The materials should NEVER be mixed, especially solid and liquid chemicals, to prevent harmful chemical reactions from occurring. Toxic gasses can be produced by mixing chemicals and if inhaled can be fatal.

Chemicals should never be added directly to the pool while it is open for use! In most cases , time should be allowed for the chemicals to circulate throughout the pool before allowing swimmers to enter.
